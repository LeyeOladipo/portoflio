About

I'm a product designer with five years of experience building systems at the intersection of complexity and clarity. My work spans financial governance platforms, SaaS tools for UK markets, and enterprise dashboards serving multi-role organisations — environments where a misunderstood flow or an ambiguous affordance has real consequences.

I don't design screens. I design systems — the logic behind role-based access, the hierarchy within a financial approval workflow, the information architecture that lets a district officer and a national auditor share the same platform without confusion.

I work closely with product managers, engineers, and stakeholders to ensure design decisions are grounded in data, business constraints, and user behaviour — not aesthetic preference. I'm most effective in ambiguous problem spaces where structure has to be invented, not inherited.
